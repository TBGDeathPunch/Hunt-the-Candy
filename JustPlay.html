Change As You Will

Introduction:

Change As You Will is a game that is all about the player's own choice of interaction with what the game has presented. The player is on their own little journey with their character and they must help guide him their each of the stages of adventure to find out what this new world is like and if he can even go home. Once you have had your fill of the adventure you can learn more about the levels that your player is adventuring through and also read his own story of the adventure just click the Level Guide.
